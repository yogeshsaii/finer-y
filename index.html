ABOUT US

Yogesh, the brilliant founder of Finer-y, hails from the coastal city of Vishakapatnam. With a deep passion for fashion and a relentless drive for creativity, Yogesh embarked on a remarkable journey to establish a brand that redefines elegance and sophistication.
As a brand, Finer-y represents the epitome of style and individuality. It offers meticulously curated collections that cater to a wide spectrum of tastes, ensuring that everyone can find their own unique expression within the brand's offerings. With a focus on sustainable practices and ethical production, Finer-y seeks to make a positive impact on the fashion industry. From the carefully sourced fabrics to the impeccably designed flagship stores, every aspect of the brand reflects a vision of creating a holistic and empowering fashion experience.
